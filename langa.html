About Me

I am Madubela Langa Monelisi, and most people call me "Mane". I was born on April 30, 2000, in Carletonville, Johannesburg. I grew up in Mthatha, Eastern Cape. I started schooling at Lutoli J.S.S. and Bensile J.S.S., and I attended Attwell Madala High School. After matriculation, I was accepted into Walter Sisulu University, where I pursued a Diploma in Information Communication Technology in Business Analysis.

I completed an online course in Data Science. I worked at Paper Jam Solutions, where I gained knowledge about fixing and operating printing machines. I also worked at Lutoli J.S.S. as an E-cadre. I have a passion for playing the keyboard, which I find therapeutic. I come from a supportive family of five, including my mother Mamtshawe and my three brothers. I'm the youngest in the family.
